Faculty of Information Studies

Faculty of Information Science Building

The Faculty of Information Studies combines (i) strengths in the stewardship and curation of cultural heritage (libraries, archives, and museums) with (ii) leadership in the future of information practice, as society is transformed by the rise of digital technologies. It pursues these goals from a progressive, inclusive, multicultural vantage point—as befits Canada’s role in the global economy. This blend of commitments will position the Faculty as the première information school with respect to issues of digital heritage, cultural curation, information policy, and associated information-intensive practice.
